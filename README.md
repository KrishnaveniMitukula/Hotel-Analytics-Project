# Travel, Tourism & Hospitality - Customer Retention and Dynamic Pricing Analysis

## Executive Problem Statement
In the highly competitive travel and hospitality sector, agencies and hotel chains suffer from significant revenue leakage due to unoptimized pricing and unpredictable customer cancellations. Traditional booking systems lack the predictive intelligence to anticipate when a user is likely to cancel a reservation or to adjust room rates dynamically based on fluctuating demand and seasonality.

## Business Objectives
The objective of this project is to analyze historical booking datasets to uncover the primary drivers of customer cancellations (churn) and to explore seasonal demand trends. By deploying Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and basic predictive modeling, this project provides the data foundation necessary for a dynamic pricing engine and targeted customer retention campaigns.

## Methodologies Used
* **Week 1:** Data Acquisition, Cleaning, and Feature Engineering (Pandas, NumPy)
* **Week 2:** Exploratory Data Analysis & Statistical Testing (Seaborn, Matplotlib)
* **Week 3:** Baseline Predictive Churn Modeling (Scikit-Learn)
* **Week 4:** Final Presentation and Dashboards (Power BI / Tableau)
